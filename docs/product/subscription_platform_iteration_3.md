# ETF Quant Subscription Platform PRD - Iteration 3

## Product Question

What would make a user trust a paid strategy enough to start a trial?

## Customer Finding From Iteration 2

Personalized recommendation improves comprehension, but payment still requires trust. The app must make verification, sample-out performance, live tracking, and creator credibility visible before subscription.

## Iteration 3 Scope

1. Trust score
   - Show a compact strategy trust score based on reproducibility, sample-out test, live tracking, and disclosure quality.

2. Verification timeline
   - Show key milestones: backtest reproduced, walk-forward passed, live signal tracking started, latest review date.

3. Creator profile
   - Show creator bio, years of experience, and platform review status.

4. Trial confidence
   - Show a clear 7-day trial CTA and what happens during the trial.
   - Make risk disclosure visible near the CTA.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Detail page explains why the strategy is allowed on the marketplace.
- User can distinguish verified strategies from weaker candidates.
- Creator credibility is visible without leaving the app.
- The customer assessment can plausibly reach "good enough for a local demo".
